Aeterna

Aeterna is a service website that intelligently checks users' social media outlets and analyzes them for dangerous or suicidal behavior.


In many cases, suicide is hard to prevent because few are paying attention to the victims' social media or notice the victims' cries for help. In addition, few can even interperet the warning signs in online messages while signals in real life are difficult to determine as well. While many may overlook these symptoms in victims' online profiles, Aeterna seeks to both to interpret and bring to light dangerous social cues, as well as suggest taking steps towards counseling or support for the victims.
